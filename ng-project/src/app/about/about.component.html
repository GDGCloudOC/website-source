Who we are

We are a community-run group that meets once a month to learn and discuss the Google Cloud Platform and related technologies. Anyone may attend regardless of experience or affiliation.

Disclaimer: GDG Cloud OC is an independent group; our activities and the opinions expressed here should in no way be linked to Google, the corporation. To learn more about the GDG program, visit https://developers.google.com/groups/

When do we meet?

Meetings typically occur on the fourth Tuesday of every month at either Google Irvine or Neudesic HQ. However time and place is always subject to change, so always be sure check the event details page to confirm. Our meetings are free and open to all, but we do have limited space so please RSVP to reserve your spot at each meeting. We are an inclusive group, and anyone may attend regardless of experience or affiliation.

What is GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, Chrome, Drive, and Google Cloud platforms, to product APIs like the Cast API, Maps API, and YouTube API.

A GDG can take many forms -- from just a few people getting together to watch our latest video, to large gatherings with demos and tech talks, to events like code sprints and hackathons. However, at the core, GDGs are focused on developers and technical content, and the core audience should be developers.
